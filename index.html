Patrick Lancaster

I am a CSE Ph.D student working with Siddhartha Srinivasa and Joshua Smith at the University of Washington. I graduated with a B.S in Electrical Engineering and a B.S in Applied Mathematics from the University of Washington in 2014. My current research is centered on the developement of new sensors for robot end-effectors and how they can be effectively used in robot perception and manipulation. Some of my research interests are sensor fusion, machine learning, and computer vision.
